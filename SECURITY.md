# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Report suspected security issues by email to [staros@staroslabs.dev](mailto:staros@staroslabs.dev).

Include:

- the affected repository or service
- a short description of the issue
- steps to reproduce or validate it
- impact if known

Do not open public issues for vulnerabilities that could expose users,
infrastructure, or private systems.

## Response Expectations

- Initial acknowledgement target: within 3 business days
- Status updates: provided as the investigation progresses
- Remediation: handled according to severity and operational risk

## Scope

This policy covers repositories and shared organization assets owned by `Staros-Labs`.
